食物保鲜盒
Teeter-Top

项目所属/Project Affiliation： Restobac
项目时间/Project Time： 2023.03 - 2023.11
项目状态/Project Status： 已上市 On the market

为法国一家公共厨房而设计的送餐保鲜盒，旨在服务社区中的老人们。
是我认为非常有意义的一个项目。
Meal delivery crisper designed for a public kitchen in France to serve the elderly in the community.
It's a program that I think is very meaningful.

通过与客户充分的沟通，我们了解，洞察到了一些值得改进的方向。
公共厨房面临的大量清洗，老年人面临的盖子开合苦难，以及运输过程中的泄露，
我们将在设计中尝试解决这些问题。
By fully communicating with our clients, we understand, and gain insight into some directions for improvement.
The large amount of cleaning faced by public kitchens, the struggle faced by the elderly to open and close the lids, and the leakage during transportation have to be improved.
We will try to address these issues in our design.

最终，我重新设计了一个斜面，
打开盖子的行为变成了向下按压，更容易施力。
Eventually, I redesigned a beveled.
The act of opening the lid becomes a downward press, making it easier to apply force.

即使用下巴，也可以打开盖子。
The lid can be opened even with the chin.

为了满足大量运输，盒子依旧可以稳定的堆叠。
The boxes remain stable and stackable in order to accommodate large shipments.
