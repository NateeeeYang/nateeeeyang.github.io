可自由组合的玻璃套装
Free Combination of Glassware Sets

服务公司/Company： IKOO Glass
项目时间/Project Time： 2023.04 - 2023.05
项目状态/Project Status: 已上市 On the market

Good Design Award 2023(Chicago Athenaeum)

一组可以自由组合使用的玻璃餐具。
A set of glass tableware that can be freely combined.

边缘的凹口，提供了放置的”Affordance”，在无意识间，餐者会寻找它，并将餐具放在它上面。
The notch in the rim provides an "Affordance" for placement, which the diner unconsciously seeks out and places the utensils on top of.

玻璃的内部设计了1°的斜面，蘸料，汤汁或是油水，自然的聚集在一侧，以便人们更好的享用食物。
The interior of the glass is designed with a 1° bevel, so that dips, soups or oils naturally gather on one side for better food enjoyment.

用户可以根据饮食习惯任意组合搭配使用，适用于各种各样的饮食文化习惯。
The user can use any combination according to dietary habits.
The modular design is suitable for a wide range of food cultures.

在极简主义设计中，蕴含了其作为餐具的多样性。
In the minimalist design lies its versatility as a tableware.
