由于疫情的影响，家中的人们更加关注自己的体温等生命体征与健康卫生状况。
本款耳温枪采用红外感应，配有一次性的耳套，并拥有弹出耳套的扳机，防止交叉感染与医疗废物二次污染。
Due to the impact of the epidemic, people at home are paying more attention to their body temperature and other vital signs and health conditions.
This ear thermometer uses infrared induction, is equipped with disposable earmuffs, and has a trigger to eject the earmuffs to prevent cross-infection and secondary pollution of medical waste.
