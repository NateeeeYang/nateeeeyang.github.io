由于湖绿色高硼硅玻璃的材料成熟，以及对亚洲饮食习惯的研究，从而设计的一套玻璃餐具。
A set of glass tableware designed as a result of the material maturity of lake green high borosilicate glass and research into Asian eating habits.

人类文明的第一个碗，便是合上捧起的双手，
第一个设计，也从一个正好与手掌合拢般大小的碗开始。
试图通过水波纹的具象化，
寻找先祖们双手捧起泉水的时刻。
The first bowl of human civilization was a bowl that closed over cupped hands.
The first design, too, began with a bowl exactly the size of a closed palm.
Through the visualization of water ripples,
we try to find the moment when our ancestors held the spring in their hands.
