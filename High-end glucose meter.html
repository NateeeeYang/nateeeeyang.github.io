高端款血糖仪
High-end Glucose Meter

项目所属/Project Affiliation：： 鱼跃医疗-Yuwell Group
项目时间/Project Time： 2022.03 - 2022.05
项目状态/Project Status: 已上市 On the market

首款定位高端的血糖仪。
深入洞察用户体验，鼓励用户使用退出按键，以避免医疗污染。创新性的将推条键设计在了正面。
在设计语义上，采用了更加方正的形态，进一步塑造专业感。
定义了高端系列的设计语言，之后的一系列设计均做了延续设计。
Deep insight into the user experience encourages users to use the Eject Button to avoid medical contamination.
Innovative design of the Eject Button on the front side.
In terms of design semantics, a more square form has been adopted to further shape the sense of professionalism.
Defining the design language of the high-end series, a series of subsequent designs were made to continue the design.
