笔式血糖仪
Portable Blood Glucose Meter

项目时间/Project Time： 2020.12 - 2022.6
所在公司/Company： 鱼跃医疗-Yuwell Group

定位便携的血糖仪。小巧的体积相比传统血糖仪，更易于携带；
简便的操作同样适合初次接触血糖产品的用户使用。
Locate a portable blood glucose meter. Compared with traditional blood glucose meters, the compact size is easier to carry;
The simple operation is also suitable for users who are new to blood glucose products.

无意识的引导是本产品的设计理念。锥状造型清晰的将用户视觉引导到试纸口的位置；
机身让用户自然而然的像笔一样握住产品。
Unconscious guidance is the design philosophy of this product. The tapered shape clearly guides the user to the position of the test strip;
The body allows users to naturally hold the product like a pen.

一个倾斜的角度，帮助用户更好的读取数值。
A sloping angle to help users read the value more easily.

【概念想法】筛选草图，挑选出两种潜在的设计形式，并进行验证。

【确定方向】挑选出合理的设计形式，进行更加深入的探索与取舍，期间开始考虑成本与生产上可能遇到的问题。
【Determine the direction】Select a reasonable design form, conduct more in-depth exploration and trade-offs,
and start to consider the cost and possible problems in production.
