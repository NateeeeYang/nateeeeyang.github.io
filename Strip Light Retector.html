带状光检影器
Strip Light Retector

项目所属/Project Affiliation： 鱼跃医疗-Yuwell Group
项目时间/Project Time： 2022.06 - 2022.09
项目状态/Project Status： 初期概念 Initial concept

为眼科医生进行眼部预检的检影镜，项目负责人。
旨在便捷医护人员的操作，降低使用的疲劳感，提升充电体验，减轻日常的工作负担。
Project Leader for a detector mirror for ophthalmologists to perform eye pre-screening.
It aims to facilitate the operation of healthcare professionals, reduce fatigue of use, enhance the charging experience and reduce the burden of daily work.
